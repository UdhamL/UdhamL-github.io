BIO

Hello, I am Udham Lalli.

I am currently a student at Simon Fraser University studying mechatronics systems engineering with a minor in computer science. As a child I was always fascinated by how things work, taking them apart and putting them back together to see how all of the small parts come together to make a function design. Also, in my final year of high school I took my first programming course and since then I have been obsessed with engineering and programming. The ability to think of a design or an idea for a project and then make it into reality is one that I will always cherish. It is my goal to one day build my own company centered around the intersection between good physical design and elegant programming.

My other interests include film and tv, exercise, boardgames, martial arts and swimming
